The Legend

Who Are the 2 Bards?

In a realm of dungeons, dark wizards, and deeply questionable decisions, Bardo and Bardidocious roam the land armed with lutes, questionable battle strategies, and an unshakeable belief in the power of a good riff.

Part adventure, part comedy, wholly bardic — 2 Bards is a cartoon series, web comic, and live theatrical experience following two musician-adventurers through a world of pixel art mayhem, folk-metal fusion, and fantasy chaos.

From the lair of the evil wizard Mantis to the mysterious hut of Baba Yaga, every quest delivers new riffs and new disasters — often at the same time.

Bardo
The Headbanger Bard
Reckless, rhythmic, and forever getting attacked by owlbears. Bardo channels raw guitar energy into battle — with mixed results.
1
Bardidocious
The Theatrical Maestro
The more "refined" of the two, Bardidocious believes every encounter deserves a dramatic overture. His lute never misses a cue.
2
Mantis
The Villain
The evil wizard behind the dark castle that looms over the kingdom. Treacherous, theatrical, and deeply annoyed by bardic music. His castle has been described as "diabolical — like an octopus made of stone."

The Lore

Welcome to Bardsworld

👑

The Royal Castle

Where King and Princess Elianna hold court. Noble, grand, and frequently disrupted by the uninvited sound of lutes.

🧙

Mantis — The Dark Wizard

The series' central villain. His castle, described as a "diabolical octopus," looms over the kingdom. He is actively annoyed by folk music.

🏚️

Baba Yaga's Swamp

The mysterious witch of the marshes. Her hut stands on chicken legs. She eats people she dislikes. Bardo knocks anyway.

🌙

The Open Road

Most of Bardsworld is discovered on foot, campfire to campfire, with nightly serenades to lull the sun to sleep.

🎸

The Music of Bardsworld

8-bit chiptune meets folk-metal fusion. Every region has its own sound — composed and animated by Peter Nickell.

Live Theater
